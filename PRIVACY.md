Privacy Policy

Roster Champ respects your privacy. This app stores roster data locally and,
when you enable cloud sync, in your AWS account. We do not sell user data.

Data We Process
- Account details (email, name) for authentication.
- If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google profile email and basic
  profile name from Google OAuth for authentication and account linking.
- Roster data, events, and preferences you enter.
- Device and session data for security and sync.

How We Use Data
- Provide roster features, sync, and analytics.
- Improve reliability and security.

Data Sharing
- Data is stored in your AWS environment. Access is controlled by your
  authentication settings and roles.

Retention
- You control your data. You can export or delete rosters at any time.

Contact
- For privacy questions, open an issue in the repository.
